1. Go to https://cal.com and sign up (free)
2. Connect your Google Calendar
3. Create event type with settings below
4. Share link: `cal.com/max-ghenis-policyengine/pose-ecosystem-interview`

## Cal.com Configuration

In Cal.com, set these as your available hours:

**General availability (default):**
- Mon-Fri: 3:00pm - 6:00pm ET (interview time after POSE training)
- Buffer time: 15 minutes before/after

**Connected calendars:**
- Your Google Calendar (blocks busy times automatically)

**Booking limits:**
- Max 4 interviews per day
- Min 2 hours notice for new bookings
