# PolicyEngine POSE Phase 1

NSF Pathways to Enable Open-Source Ecosystems (POSE) Phase 1 program materials for PolicyEngine.

## Program overview

- **Program**: I-Corps™ for POSE Winter 2026
- **Goal**: Complete 100 Ecosystem Discovery Interviews
- **Duration**: ~7 weeks (Kickoff → Finale)

## Interview pacing

| Milestone | Target interviews |
|-----------|------------------|
| End of Kickoff Week | 10 |
| Weekly Session 1 | 25 |
| Weekly Session 2 | 40 |
| Weekly Session 3 | 55 |
| Weekly Session 4 | 70 |
| Weekly Session 5 | 85 |
| Finale Presentation | 100 |

## Stakeholder types

Interview across all ecosystem segments:
- **Users** - People who use PolicyEngine directly
- **Supporters** - Funders, advocates, policy champions
- **Contributors** - Developers, data contributors, documentation writers
- **Competitors** - Alternative tools and approaches
- **Distributors** - Organizations that embed/redistribute PolicyEngine
- **Partners** - Organizations that integrate with or complement PolicyEngine

## Required deliverables

Weekly slide presentations covering:
1. **Team** - Team members and roles
2. **Thesis** - OSE thesis statement (AdLib format)
3. **Assumptions** - 2-3 riskiest assumptions to test through interviews
4. **Interview Log** - Chronological list with name, role, org, dates, notes link
5. **Interview Records** - Detailed notes from each interview
6. **Ecosystem Map** - Visual showing interview coverage across segments
7. **Open Source Ecosystem Canvas** - Updated weekly based on discovery
8. **Ecosystem Segment Profiles** - Deep dives into each segment
9. **Impact** - Impact thesis and metrics
10. **Governance** - Current and future governance models
11. **Sustainability** - Funding and operational sustainability
12. **Partnerships** - Distribution and integration partners
13. **Timeline** - Milestones during and after POSE grant
14. **Team Charter** - Shared commitments, roles, agreements
